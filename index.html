Corona virus

When Corona virus was discovered in China........

One day we heard the news about Corona virus (covid 19) in India . We didn't believe that.

When Corona virus came to India........

After some days,they said Corona virus is spreading all over India.we didn't believe this also.Some days later our Indian prime minister said everyone should stay at home. Some people obeyed this and some didn't .

When we came to know that it was real........

Days passed. They blocked the streets police force were standing everywhere.Then one wierd thing happened.Our street was also blocked!!!.Volunteers came and supplied essential items.

Main thing........

There is a way to fight Corona virus.It is to keep us clean.They told various do's and dont's. here are some.

Do's

  1. Wash your hands with soap and water.
  2. Use hand sanitizers.
  3. Don't touch your face without washing your hands.
  4. Maintain social distancing.

Don'ts

  1. Going out without mask.
  2. Not maintaining social distancing.
  3. Sneezing and coughing without covering the mouth.
  4. Going out unnecessarily.
